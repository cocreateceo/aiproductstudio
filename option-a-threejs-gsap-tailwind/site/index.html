Founder FAQ

The things founders actually ask.

How fast can we ship the first version?
Two weeks from kick-off, every time. We shape scope to fit the window — the smallest credible MVP that proves the thesis, deployed and in the hands of real users.
What's the catch with $0 upfront?
No catch — we share the risk because we share the upside. The exact split depends on the project; we walk through it on the first call.
Do I own the code and the brand?
Yes. You own the product, the code, the data, and the brand. We're the co-founder partner — not the owner.
What stack do you build on?
Production AI-native stacks: Next.js or vanilla front-ends, Node / Python services, AWS (Lambda + S3 + CloudFront), and the right AI APIs for the job (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, embeddings, etc.).
What happens after launch?
We keep going. Iterate, grow, harden — the same team, the same shared-upside model. We're aligned on the long game, not a hand-off.
Does my idea qualify for the model?
If it's a real founder-led product with a real audience, almost always yes. We'll tell you on the first call if it's not a fit, no theatrics.
